Getting to Taniti

Most travelers arrive to Taniti by plane. But several cruise ships can bring you too.

Arrive by Plane

Taniti is served by a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes. Taniti is in the process of expanding the airport so larger jets will be able to land on the island within the next few years.

Arrive by Ship

Book passage on the many small cruise ships dock in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week.

Getting Around Taniti

Moving around Taniti is easy. There are public buses, taxis, and the Taniti City is very walkable and bikable.

Public Buses

Public buses serve Taniti City and run from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. every day. Private buses serve the rest of the island.

Taxis

Taxis are available in Taniti City, and rental cars can be rented from a local rental agency near the airport.

Walking and Biking

Bikes and helmets are available to rent from several vendors (helmets are required by law). Taniti City is fairly flat and very walkable. Many tourists stay in the area surrounding Merriton Landing: this area is easy to explore on foot.
