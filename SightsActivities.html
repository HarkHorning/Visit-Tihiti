Explore Taniti

While the island has an area of less than 500 square miles, the terrain is varied and includes sandy beaches, a small but safe harbor, tropical rainforests, and a mountainous interior that includes a small, active volcano.

Taniti City

Most tourists spend most of their time in Taniti City, which boasts native architecture and nearby white, sandy beaches that encircle Yellow Leaf Bay.

Yellow Leaf Bay

Near Taniti City, Yellow Leaf Bay has many white, sandy beaches.

Popular activities:

Most people visit Taniti to enjoy the beaches, explore the rainforest, and to visit the volcano. However, there are other many activities to fill your days. These include.

  • Local history museum.
  • Chartered fishing tours.
  • snorkeling.
  • Taking helicopter rides.
  • Zip-lining in the rainforest.
  • visiting several pubs, including a microbrewery.
  • Go dancing at a new dance club.
  • Seeing a movie.
  • Playing at an arcade.
  • Visiting art galleries.
  • Bowling.
  • Nine-hole golf course should be operational by next year.

Many of these activities are located in Merriton Landing, which is a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.
